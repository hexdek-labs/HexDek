# Hat Choice Gaps — Pilot Decision Points Needing Intelligence

**Flagged:** 2026-04-18 by 7174n1c
**Status:** Documented — engine correctly exposes choice points, Hats need smarter logic

These are NOT engine bugs. The engine offers the choice. The Hats make dumb decisions.
When building smarter Hats, these are the decision points that need intelligence.

## Proliferate Choices
- Which permanents/players to choose for proliferation
- Skip opponent Sagas (don't advance chapters)
- Skip own -1/-1 counters
- Strategic proliferate of own +1/+1 vs opponent poison

## Combat Choices
- Attack target in multiplayer (political, threat-based)
- Block assignment order (strategic damage ordering)
- "May block additional" — split blocking decisions
- When to NOT attack (hold back for defense)

## Spell Choices
- Modal spells ("choose one or more") — evaluate board for best mode
- "Choose a creature type" — match your board composition
- "Choose a color" — match opponent's threats
- X cost — strategic underpay vs full spend
- Optional additional costs ("you may pay {2} more")

## Sacrifice Choices
- Which creature to sacrifice (death trigger value, recursion targets)
- Grave Pact opponent sacrifice (from THEIR perspective)
- "Sacrifice an artifact" — protect key pieces

## Discard Choices
- Graveyard synergy (WANT to discard reanimation targets)
- Hold answers, discard redundant cards
- Madness — discard for value

## Trigger Ordering
- Same-controller simultaneous triggers — strategic ordering
- "Whenever ~ deals damage" before/after other triggers

## Tutor Choices
- Find combo piece vs answer vs ramp based on game state
- Threat assessment for removal tutoring

## Counter Placement
- Where to put +1/+1 counters among multiple creatures
- "Choose a counter type" — strategic selection
- Distribute counters vs stack on one creature
